Our services

HVAC

We have a track record of delivering large and complex HVAC installation projects on schedule and within budget. We utilise the latest in technologies to deliver industry leading innovation, sizing, installation and commissioning on projects. Our involvement does not stop at the completion of the project. Our offering integrates to cover the full building life cycle. Supported by our expert Service and Maintenance business, our association with these buildings continue by servicing and maintaining and preserving these valuable assets for many years after.

We utilise the latest technologies including Building Information Modelling and Integrated Solutions Software to validate our initiatives and our processes that have been refined over many years of experience.

We employ experienced, qualified refrigeration technicians and engineers who are trained to view each element as a part of a complex system that is required to function efficiently and reliably.

Our Service include

  • Installation of Chillers.
  • Chilled water piping.
  • Package units (DX systems).
  • VRVs systems & refrigeration piping
  • AHU, fresh air units, VAVs and FCUs installation and its respective ducting works.
  • Cladding of ducting and piping works.
  • Control valve stations & BTU meter installation works.
  • Energy management advice.
  • Extract systems – installation of roof mounted & inline fans with ducting systems.
  • Fire rated duct works for stairways and parking areas.

Service and maintenance of HVAC systems includes

  • Air Cooled Central Plant Systems, Refrigeration Systems, and DX Unitary Equipment.
  • Upgrades and Fitout works.
  • Breakdown and emergency repairs.
  • Equipment appraisal.
  • Longevity of equipment.
  • Temporary cooling – hire equipment including delivery, professional installation and removal
